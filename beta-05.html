β-VAE (β = 0.5) — Reconstruction-Focused Variational Model

When β < 1, the model assigns less weight to the KL divergence term, effectively prioritizing reconstruction accuracy over latent space regularization. This configuration bridges the gap between deterministic autoencoders and fully regularized VAEs.

Key Insight: β = 0.5 produces the best VAE reconstructions with Test VAE Loss of 0.129, but at the cost of a less organized latent space with higher KL divergence (9.79).
