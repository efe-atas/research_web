β-VAE (β = 4.0) — Disentanglement-Focused Training

When β > 1, the model places stronger emphasis on the KL divergence term, encouraging the latent distribution to closely match the standard normal prior. This promotes disentanglement.

Key Insight: β = 4.0 achieves the lowest KL divergence (2.64) and the most regularized latent space (σ mean = 0.963), but has the highest reconstruction error.
